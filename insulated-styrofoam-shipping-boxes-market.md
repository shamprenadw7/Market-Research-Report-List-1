<p><p>Insulated Styrofoam shipping boxes include various types, primarily distinguished by their materials. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) is lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for temperature-sensitive items. Expanded Polypropylene (EPP) offers higher durability and resistance to impact, making it suitable for more fragile goods. Expanded Polyurethane (EPU) provides superior insulation and energy efficiency, perfect for specialized applications. Expanded Polyethylene (EPE) is flexible and offers good cushioning properties, often used for custom packaging solutions. Each material serves unique needs in temperature control and protection during shipping.</p></p>
