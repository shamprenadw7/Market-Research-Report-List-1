<p><strong>The Constant Velocity Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Type</li><li>Fork Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The constant velocity joints (CV joints) market is primarily segmented into two types: ball type and fork type. Ball type CV joints feature spherical balls that allow for smooth motion and flexibility at various angles, making them ideal for front-wheel drive vehicles. In contrast, fork type CV joints use a forked design to accommodate larger angles and higher torque loads, typically found in heavier vehicles and off-road applications. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient power transfer and enhanced vehicle performance.</p></p>
