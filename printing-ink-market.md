<p><p>The printing ink market comprises several types, each tailored for specific applications. **Offset inks** are widely used for commercial printing due to their high quality and efficiency. **Gravure inks** are ideal for high-speed printing on flexible substrates, often found in packaging. **Flexo inks** are popular in label and packaging printing, known for their versatility and quick drying. **Screen inks** are utilized in specialty applications, offering vibrant colors on various surfaces. Other inks cover niche markets, including digital and specialty applications.</p></p>
