<p><p>Hydraulic operating tables are essential in surgical settings, offering adjustable positions for optimal patient access. The market is divided into two main types: electro-hydraulic and manual-hydraulic operating tables. Electro-hydraulic tables utilize electric controls for precise adjustments, allowing for seamless operation with minimal physical effort, enhancing efficiency in the operating room. In contrast, manual-hydraulic tables rely on mechanical pumps for height and position adjustments, offering a cost-effective solution but requiring more physical strength and effort from the user.</p></p>
