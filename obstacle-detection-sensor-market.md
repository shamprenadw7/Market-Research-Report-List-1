Laser scanners deploy laser beams to create precise 3D maps of environments. Radar sensors utilize radio waves to detect object distance and speed, effective in various weather conditions. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves to detect nearby objects, commonly used in automotive applications. Each type enhances safety and navigation by providing critical real-time data.</p></p>
