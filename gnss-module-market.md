<p><strong>The GNSS Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Constellation</li><li>Multi-Frequency</li><li>Augmentation</li><li>Differential</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS module market comprises several types: Multi-Constellation modules leverage multiple satellite networks like GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou for improved accuracy and reliability. Multi-Frequency modules utilize signals from various frequency bands, enhancing precision and reducing errors. Augmentation systems enhance GNSS signals with additional information for greater accuracy. Differential GNSS applies corrections based on satellite and ground station data to minimize location errors. Other categories may include technologies like inertial navigation or chipsets tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
