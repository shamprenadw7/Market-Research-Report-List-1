<p><p>The printed image sensors market encompasses various printing technologies, each serving distinct applications. Inkjet printing utilizes tiny droplets of ink for high-resolution images, ideal for customizable prints. Flexographic printing employs flexible plates for efficient, high-speed production, commonly used in packaging. Screen printing involves pushing ink through a mesh screen, suitable for thick applications like textiles and signage. Other methods may include digital printing or gravure, catering to niche requirements. Each type contributes uniquely to the overall printed image sensors landscape.</p></p>
