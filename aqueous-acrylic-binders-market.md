<p><p>The aqueous acrylic binders market comprises various types, including solution type, dispersion type, emulsion type, and 100% polymer liquid type. Solution type binders are characterized by their solubility in water, offering easy application and fast drying. Dispersion type binders feature fine polymer particles suspended in water for effective binding and stability. Emulsion type binders consist of oil droplets dispersed in water, providing excellent adhesion. Lastly, 100% polymer liquid binders contain no solvents, ensuring eco-friendliness and high-performance characteristics.</p></p>
