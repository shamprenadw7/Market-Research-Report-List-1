<p><p>The gravel cleaner market features two primary types: siphon-style gravel cleaners and electric gravel cleaners. Siphon-style cleaners utilize a manual suction mechanism, using a tube to siphon debris and debris from the substrate, making them cost-effective and easy to use without power sources. In contrast, electric gravel cleaners employ motorized pumps to facilitate cleaning, offering greater efficiency and ease for larger aquariums. Both types cater to various user preferences, enhancing aquarium maintenance and ensuring a healthy aquatic environment.</p></p>
