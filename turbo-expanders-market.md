<p><strong>The Turbo-expanders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Turbo-expander</li><li>Axial Turbo-expander</li><li>Radial-Axial Turbo-expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turbo-expander market comprises three main types: radial, axial, and radial-axial turbo-expanders. Radial turbo-expanders utilize centrifugal force for gas expansion and are efficient in smaller applications. Axial turbo-expanders feature a design where gas flows parallel to the axis, offering high efficiency for large-scale processes. Radial-axial turbo-expanders combine both designs, providing versatility and improved performance across various industrial applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs, influencing their adoption in sectors like natural gas processing and refrigeration.</p></p>
