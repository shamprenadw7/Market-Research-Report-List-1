<p><p>The agricultural UAV market comprises three main types: Fixed-wing, Multi-rotor, and Hybrid UAVs. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed for long-range missions, offering greater endurance and efficiency for large farms. Multi-rotor UAVs provide remarkable maneuverability and are ideal for detailed crop monitoring and precision spraying. Hybrid UAVs combine features of both types, allowing for extended flight times while maintaining the agility of multi-rotors. Each type serves distinct agricultural needs, enhancing efficiency and productivity in farming practices.</p></p>
