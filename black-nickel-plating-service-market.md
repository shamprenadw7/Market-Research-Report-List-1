<p><p>The Black Nickel Plating Service market is divided into two main types: electrochemically deposited and chemically deposited methods. Electrochemically deposited plating involves an electrochemical process where nickel is deposited onto a substrate using electrical current, providing a durable finish with excellent adhesion. In contrast, chemically deposited black nickel involves a chemical reduction process, allowing a uniform coating without requiring electrical power. Both methods are used to enhance corrosion resistance and aesthetic appeal in various industries, including automotive and electronics.</p></p>
