<p><p>The flat panel display market features various technologies such as LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) utilizes liquid crystals sandwiched between glass layers, providing efficient energy use and slim designs. LED (Light Emitting Diode) displays, often considered an enhancement of LCD, use LED backlighting for brighter images and better contrast. OLED (Organic Light Emitting Diode) offers superior picture quality with deeper blacks and wider viewing angles by using organic compounds that emit light individually. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
