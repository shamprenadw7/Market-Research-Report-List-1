<p><p>Hydrocarbon Ceramic Laminates in the market are categorized into three main types: Common FR-4, Mid-Tg FR-4, and High-Tg FR-4. Common FR-4 is widely used for standard applications due to its cost-effectiveness and versatility. Mid-Tg FR-4 offers improved thermal resistance, making it suitable for moderate temperature applications. High-Tg FR-4 features superior thermal stability, ideal for demanding electronic environments subjected to higher temperatures and stress, thus enhancing performance and reliability in high-performance devices.</p></p>
