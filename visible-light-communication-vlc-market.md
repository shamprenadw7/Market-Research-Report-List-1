<p><p>Visible Light Communication (VLC) is a wireless communication technology that utilizes visible light for data transmission. By modulating light emitted from LEDs, VLC enables high-speed communication without requiring radio frequencies, making it a potential solution for environments where radio waves are less effective, such as in hospitals and aircraft. The technology is gaining traction due to its advantages in terms of safety, security, and energy efficiency.</p><p>The Visible Light Communication (VLC) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-speed data transfer and advancements in LED technology. Key sectors influencing market growth include healthcare, automotive, and smart lighting systems. The proliferation of smart cities and the Internet of Things (IoT) initiatives are also pushing VLC into the spotlight as a complementary technology to existing wireless systems, addressing bandwidth constraints faced by traditional Wi-Fi networks. Moreover, innovations in optical wireless communication techniques are enhancing the reliability and performance of VLC systems. As businesses and consumers seek more efficient and cost-effective communication methods, the VLC market is poised for significant expansion in the coming years.</p></p>
