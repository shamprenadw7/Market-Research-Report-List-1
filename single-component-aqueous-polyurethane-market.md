<p><strong>The Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion</li><li>Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane market consists primarily of two types: Aqueous Polyurethane Dispersion (APD) and Aqueous Polyurethane Emulsion (APEm). APD is a stable mixture of polyurethane polymers in water, offering excellent adhesion, flexibility, and hardness, making it ideal for coatings and sealants. In contrast, APEm involves the dispersion of polyurethane in water with surfactants, providing enhanced stability and performance for applications like textiles and adhesives. Both variants are environmentally friendly alternatives to solvent-based products.</p></p>
