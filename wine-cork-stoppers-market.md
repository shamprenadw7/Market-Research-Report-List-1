<p><ul><li>Natural Cork Stopper</li><li>Agglomerated Cork Stopper</li><li>Capsulated Cork Stoppers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wine cork stoppers market comprises three main types: natural cork stoppers, agglomerated cork stoppers, and capsulated cork stoppers. Natural cork stoppers are made from the bark of cork oak trees, offering superior quality and breathability. Agglomerated cork stoppers are produced from granulated cork, providing a cost-effective alternative with moderate sealing properties. Capsulated cork stoppers feature a plastic or aluminum coating, enhancing durability and ensuring a tight seal, making them popular for both still and sparkling wines.</p></p>
