<p><strong>The PUR Hot Melts Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic PUR Hot Melts Adhesive</li><li>Reactive PUR Hot Melts Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PUR Hot Melts Adhesives market is primarily divided into two types: thermoplastic and reactive. Thermoplastic PUR hot melts provide a quick setting bond and are easily reworked, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and easy application. Reactive PUR hot melts, on the other hand, cure through a chemical reaction, offering superior bond strength and durability, suitable for demanding environments. Both types find applications in various industries, including automotive, woodworking, and packaging, due to their effective adhesion properties.</p></p>
