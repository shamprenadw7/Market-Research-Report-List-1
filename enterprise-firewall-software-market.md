<p><p>The Enterprise Firewall Software market is divided into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. On-Premise firewalls are installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, offering complete control and customization, but requiring significant maintenance and resources. In contrast, Cloud-Based firewalls are hosted off-site and managed by third-party providers, offering scalability, reduced management overhead, and ease of deployment. Both types aim to enhance network security, yet their deployment methods cater to different organizational needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
