<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared (SWIR) market is segmented primarily into Area Scan and Line Scan types. Area Scan cameras capture a full image at once, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed analysis of stationary or slow-moving objects, such as agricultural monitoring and security. Conversely, Line Scan cameras capture one line of pixels at a time, ideal for inspecting high-speed production processes, like in manufacturing and packaging, as they can continuously scan moving items, ensuring thorough quality control.</p></p>
