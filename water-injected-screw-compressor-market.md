<p><p>The water-injected screw compressor market features two primary types: positive displacement compressors and speed compressors. Positive displacement compressors operate by trapping a specific volume of air and compressing it, ensuring consistent output and efficiency. Speed compressors, on the other hand, adjust their speed to meet variable demand, providing flexibility in output while optimizing energy consumption. Both types leverage water injection to enhance cooling and lubrication, improving performance and extending equipment lifespan in various industrial applications.</p></p>
