<p><p>Biomass essential oil extraction equipment comprises various technologies tailored for efficient oil recovery. Super-critical CO2 extraction equipment utilizes carbon dioxide in a supercritical state to dissolve essential oils, ensuring high purity and yield without harmful solvents. Ethanol extraction equipment employs ethanol as a solvent, effectively extracting oils while maintaining quality. Hydrocarbon extraction equipment uses hydrocarbons like butane or propane, enabling efficient extraction but requiring careful management due to flammability. Each method offers unique advantages depending on the desired application and oil type.</p></p>
