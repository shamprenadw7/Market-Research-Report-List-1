<p><p>Truck Stop Electrification (TSE) involves two main market types: single-system and dual-system electrification. </p><p>Single-system electrification provides a single source of power to trucks, typically offering standard electricity for engine block heaters and auxiliary power units, enhancing efficiency and reducing idling. </p><p>Dual-system electrification combines multiple power sources, allowing trucks to access both AC and DC power options, catering to diverse needs and improving flexibility. This can accommodate various truck models and operational requirements, promoting sustainability in the freight industry.</p></p>
