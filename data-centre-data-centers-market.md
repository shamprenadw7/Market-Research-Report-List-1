<p><p>Data centers are facilities that house computer systems and associated components for data storage and management. In-house data centers are owned and operated by companies for their exclusive use. Colocation involves renting space within a facility to house servers alongside others. Wholesale data centers lease large spaces to businesses at scale. Dedicated hosting offers single-client server access, while managed hosting includes server management services. Shared hosting provides resources among multiple clients, optimizing costs but limiting control and performance.</p></p>
