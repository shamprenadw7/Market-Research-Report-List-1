<p><p>The jetting pumps market is categorized into three main types: deep well, shallow well, and convertible pumps. Deep well jetting pumps are designed for extracting water from significant depths, making them ideal for agricultural and municipal uses. Shallow well jetting pumps are suited for limited depth applications, typically found in residential settings. Convertible pumps are versatile, capable of operating in both shallow and deep well scenarios, offering flexibility for users who may need to switch between different water sourcing requirements.</p></p>
